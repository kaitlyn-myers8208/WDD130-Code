Welcome to my page! Here I hope to share the love that I have for musicals and plays and help you to develop your love for them as well!

BACKGROUND

I’ve grown up going to plays and listening to their soundtracks on any road trips with my family, so they’ve always been apart of my life. In more recent years however, I’ve been able to see one or two plays a month with my family. Theater has always been something I’ve loved, but experiencing it more thoroughly in my life has helped me grow to appreciate the work that goes into the productions and the storylines.
